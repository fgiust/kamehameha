Paired Intransitive Verbs + ている
Coppie di verbi intransitivi + ている

# Similarly, when followed by ている, transitive verbs refer to actions in progress,
# while intransitive verbs refer to states that hold true after the change takes place.

Michiko is closing the door.
Michiko sta chiudendo la porta.
みちこさんはドアを閉[し]めてい{ます|る}

The door is closed.
La porta è chiusa.
ドア{は|が}閉[し]まってい{ます|る}

Ken is turning on the light.
Ken sta accendendo la luce.
けんさん{は|が}電[でん]気[き]をつけてい{ます|る}

The light is on.
La luce è accesa.
電[でん]気[き]がついてい{ます|る}

Yui is putting a book in her bag.
Yui sta mettendo un libro nella borsa.
ゆいさんは{カバン|鞄[かばん]}に本[ほん]を入[い]れてい{ます|る}

There is a book in the bag.
C'è un libro nella borsa.
{カバン|鞄[かばん]}に本[ほん]が入[はい]ってい{ます|る}

The cat is making the shirt dirty.
Il gatto sta sporcando la camicia.
猫[ねこ]がシャツを汚[よご]してい{ます|る}

The shirt is dirty.
La camicia è sporca.
シャツが汚[よご]れてい{ます|る}

Naomi is boiling water.
Naomi sta facendo bollire l'acqua.
なおみさんはお湯[ゆ]を沸[わ]かしてい{ます|る}

The water is boiling.
L'acqua bolle.
お湯[ゆ]{が|は}沸[わ]いてい{ます|る}
